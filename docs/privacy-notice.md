# Privacy Notice (P12-4)

This Privacy Notice outlines how ScholarMe collects, processes, and protects personal data in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

## 1. Information We Collect
When you register and use ScholarMe, we collect:
- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, university ID number, course/degree.
- **Academic & Operational Data:** Tutoring sessions attended, study sets created, quiz scores, XP logs.
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, and optional push notification tokens.
- **Financial Information:** Bank details (only if you request petty cash reimbursements or liquidation).

## 2. How We Use Your Data
We use this information to:
- Verify your eligibility as a student or organization member.
- Match learners with appropriate tutors and study groups.
- Track attendance and gamification metrics.
- Process financial reimbursements internally.

## 3. Data Sharing & Third Parties
- We **do not sell** your data to third parties.
- We use Supabase (AWS/GCP) for secure cloud database hosting.
- We may export aggregated, anonymized attendance reports for university accreditation purposes.
- Any integrations with external LLMs (e.g., Gemini, Groq) use anonymized text prompts and do not include your personally identifiable information (PII).

## 4. Your Rights (Data Subject Rights)
Under the DPA, you have the right to:
- **Be Informed:** This notice serves that purpose.
- **Access:** You can request a full JSON export of your data via your Account Settings.
- **Rectification:** You can update your profile information at any time.
- **Erasure:** You can delete your account from your Account Settings. Deleting your account will immediately and permanently erase your profile and cascade to delete your sessions, logs, and messages.

## 5. Contact
If you have concerns about your privacy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact the Data Protection Officer (or equivalent administrative head) of the organization hosting this ScholarMe instance.
